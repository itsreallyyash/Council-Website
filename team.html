About Us

The Students’ Council

The Students’ Council of NMIMS MPSTME is the foundation that helps students flourish by providing a multitude of co-curricular, extracurricular, and athletic opportunities. Our vision is to harness the creative potential of students through the Sports, Culture, Social, Literature and Technical forums under its domain. We at the Students’ Council believe in providing students with valuable learning experiences and promote a sense of community.

Colloqium

a committee pertaining to the business domain that generates ideas and leaders that have the power to transform the world. They bring to us their flagship event of Stockraze and Bizneeti.

Cultural Committee

a platform to display one’s creativity and showcase their talent. They host the Cultural Night, a night of entertainment for everyone to revel in.

Editorial Board

brings together the world of art, literature and popular culture. The Editorial Project is one among the many events they produce that truly celebrates fine arts.

Research Cell

guides students to turn ideas into reality. They organise the Mentor-Mentee Program, a great depiction of their belief as a committee.

Social Impact

strives to bring about changes in society through cultivating the aspect of social responsibilities in future leaders. They established their very own Social Conclave, an event that works closely at finding solutions to social problems.

Sports Committee

is a platform for all athletes of the institute. They host Conquest and Conquer, which facilitates highlighting the sports culture among students.

Technical Committee

believes in imparting knowledge to all by encouraging technological aptitude among students and Taqneeq promotes exactly that.
