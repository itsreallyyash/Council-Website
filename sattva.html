The Technical Committee is the tech and digital arm of the Students' Council. We're the ones who provide experience of excitement, enthusiasm, and a platform to showcase one’s skills. From industrial visits to internship fairs, we strive for the holistic development of our members and event participants. Tech Comm is for those that want a head start in their engineering journey by developing their technical aptitude.

Flagship Events

Taqneeq, MPSTME’s Annual Technical Fest is a three-day celebration of technology with a rich history that witnesses a greater footfall every passing year. It is a technological marvel which provides dreamers and believers with a stage to hone their passions and not just limit themselves to bookish knowledge. With its precursor, the Tech Carnival, Taqneeq is fast becoming the paradigm of how a technical fest should be, and it is technology in its purest form.
The internship fair serves to bring together companies willing to provide an opportunity for budding engineers. The fair will provide students with much-needed industry experience to propel themselves in their technical endeavours. Apart from gaining adequate exposure to the latest technologies, they will learn how to work with a team, build interpersonal skills and manage time.
